Sailor Moon

About Sailor Moon

Sailor Moon is the alter ego of Serena Tsukino, who is a high school girl trying to balance school, friends, family and saving the world her new moon powers. Her mentor is Luna, a talking black cat from the moon, and she discovers more about herself than she thought she was ever capable of.

Sailor Moon's Characteristics

  1. She's kind to all animals.
  2. She's a bit of scaredy cat but will always come through in the end.
  3. She's also accident prone and causing messes.

This is Sailor Moon and the Sailor Scouts: Sailor Mercury, Sailor Mars, Sailor Jupitor, Sailor Venus.

Sailor Moon's Friends

  • Amy Anderson is a girl from Serena's class. She is booksmart and very tech savy. She is also quiet but that doesn't mean she's shy about her opinions. She's also a Sailor Scout, Sailor Mercury.
  • Raye Hino is a shrine maiden, who Serena meets on a school field trip. She is very focused on her goals and takes her responsiblities as a shrine maiden very seriously. She is also a Sailor Scout, Sailor Mars.
  • Lita Kino is a transfer student and is also in Serena's class. She is strong and very kind. She is interesting in cooking and gardening. She is also a Sailor Scout, Sailor Jupiter
  • Mina Aino is a Sailor Scout, Sailor Venus. She meets Sailor Moon during a battle, after it is over Mina asks to meet Sailor Moon and the other Sailor Scouts in their true forms to inform them of what she knows. She is very perky and dreams to become a singer.
